About Me

Welcome to my course page! My name is Minenhle, but most people call me Minnie. I’m proudly South African, born and raised in the vibrant city of Durban, where I live with my family. I’m currently a full-time online student at BYU–Idaho, studying Web and Software Development a field that beautifully blends my love for creativity with the power of logic and technology.

My hobbies include drawing, painting, crafting, cooking, and experimenting with hair and makeup. These creative passions have shaped how I approach software development. Whether it’s designing intuitive user interfaces or solving complex problems, I’ve found that creativity fuels innovation. Studies show that creative thinkers often excel in programming because they’re able to visualize abstract concepts, think outside the box, and approach challenges with flexibility and imagination. For me, coding is just another canvas one where I get to build tools that make life better. Beyond tech, I’m deeply passionate about empowering women and children. My dream is to one day teach, mentor, and provide resources that help them thrive especially by funding education and career opportunities that break cycles of poverty. I believe in building futures where every child can dream boldly and every woman can lead confidently.

This mission is more than a goal it’s a calling. I strive to live as a disciple of Jesus Christ, and I seek Him daily in everything I do. His love guides my choices, fuels my compassion, and strengthens me in moments of doubt. I encourage everyone to build a personal relationship with Him, because I’ve seen firsthand how transformative His light can be.“I am the light of the world. A city that is set on a hill cannot be hid.” — Matthew 5:14 “I can do all things through Christ who strengthens me.” — Philippians 4:13 Thank you for visiting my page. I’m excited to keep learning, growing, and building a life and career that reflects creativity, service, and faith.
